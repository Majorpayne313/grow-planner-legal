Delete Your Account

This page explains how users of The Growmies Planner & Hangout can request deletion of their account and associated data.

```

Users can delete their account directly inside the app at any time.

How to delete your account in the app

  1. Open The Growmies Planner & Hangout.
  2. Go to the Profile tab.
  3. Open Privacy Center.
  4. Select Delete Account.
  5. Follow the confirmation steps to permanently delete your account.

What data is deleted

When you delete your account, we delete account data associated with your user profile, including:

  • Account profile information
  • Grow profiles
  • Daily grow logs and planner data
  • Uploaded grow media connected to your account
  • Community posts and comments made by your account
  • Direct message threads and messages associated with your account
  • Notifications and app activity records connected to your account
  • AI assistant and plant scan records connected to your account
  • In-app purchase records stored by the app

Data that may be retained

Some limited information may be retained if required for legal, security, payment, fraud prevention, dispute resolution, or compliance purposes. Payment transaction records may also remain available through Apple, Google, or other payment processors according to their policies.
```
