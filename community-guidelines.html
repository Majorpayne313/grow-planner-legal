Grow Planner Community Guidelines

Grow Planner is for grow tracking, education, research, and respectful community support.

Respectful Behavior

Do not harass, bully, threaten, intimidate, impersonate, scam, or target other users.

Hate Speech

Do not attack people based on protected traits including race, ethnicity, nationality, religion, sex, gender identity, sexual orientation, disability, veteran status, caste, or serious medical condition.

Child Safety

Content involving exploitation, grooming, sexualization, abuse, or endangerment of minors is strictly prohibited and may be reported to appropriate authorities.

Illegal or Dangerous Activity

Do not use Grow Planner to sell illegal goods, arrange illegal activity, threaten harm, or provide dangerous instructions.

Reports and Moderation

Users can report posts or DM threads. Moderators may review reported content and take action including warnings, content removal, account restrictions, or bans.
