Grow Planner Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 28, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Grow Planner may collect account information such as name, email, username, profile details, subscription status, app settings, grow logs, community posts, direct messages, reports, media uploads, device push tokens, and usage activity needed to operate the app.

2. How We Use Information

We use information to provide app features, authenticate users, save grow records, display community content, send notifications, process reports, improve safety, support subscriptions, and maintain app functionality.

3. Community Content

Content you post in community areas may be visible to other users depending on the app’s privacy settings and feed design. Direct messages are private between participants but may be reviewed by authorized moderators when needed for safety, reports, abuse investigations, or policy enforcement.

4. Media and AI Features

If you use AI grow assistant or plant scanner features, text, photos, videos, or related grow information may be processed to provide AI-supported observations. Do not upload content you do not want processed for app functionality.

5. Push Notifications

If you enable notifications, we store push tokens for your device so we can send alerts for DMs, comments, reactions, follows, account notices, and app updates. You can disable notifications in the app or device settings.

6. Reports and Moderation

When content is reported, moderators may review reported posts, messages, media, account activity, and related context to investigate and take action.

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may use service providers for hosting, databases, authentication, push notifications, payments, analytics, email delivery, and AI processing where needed to operate the app.

8. Account Controls

The app may provide tools to update your profile, manage privacy settings, block users, change your password, verify email, and manage notifications.

9. Data Retention

We keep information as long as needed to provide the app, comply with obligations, resolve disputes, enforce rules, support moderation, and maintain security.

10. Children

Grow Planner is not intended for children. Child exploitation, grooming, sexualization, or abuse content is strictly prohibited and may be escalated to appropriate authorities.

11. Contact

For privacy questions, contact the app owner through official support channels provided in the app.
