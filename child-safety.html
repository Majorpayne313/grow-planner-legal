Child Safety Policy

Grow Planner has zero tolerance for child exploitation, abuse, grooming, or sexualization of minors.

Strictly Prohibited

Users may not create, upload, request, share, promote, or distribute content involving child sexual abuse material, grooming, exploitation, sexualization of minors, or endangerment of minors.

Reporting

Report suspected child safety violations immediately using the in-app report tools. Serious reports may be escalated to appropriate authorities or safety organizations where required.

Enforcement

Violations may result in immediate content removal, permanent account bans, and escalation to appropriate legal or safety channels.
