Safety and Reporting

Grow Planner provides report and block tools to help keep the community safer.

What to Report

Report abuse, bullying, harassment, hate speech, threats, scams, exploitation, unsafe behavior, or content that violates the Community Guidelines.

How to Report

Use the options menu on a community post or DM thread and choose Report. Include clear details when possible.

Blocking

Blocking helps hide users from your community feed and search. You can review blocked growmies from Privacy Center.

Moderation Review

Reports may be reviewed by moderators. Actions can include dismissal, warnings, content removal, temporary restrictions, permanent bans, or escalation where legally required.
